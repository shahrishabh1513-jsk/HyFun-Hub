About HyFun Foods

Our journey from humble beginnings to a global brand

Our Journey

In 1962, the first generation of entrepreneurs at M/s. Asandas & Sons began the trading of Potatoes and Onions. The second generation further expanded the business and by November 2015, the third generation, Mr. Haresh Karamchandani set up HyFun to produce a variety of delicacies such as French Fries, Burger Patties, Cheese Poppers etc. In a short span of 5 years HyFun became one of the most valued B2B player, catering to leading global QSR brands.

HyFun is a proud contributor to the 'Make in India' initiative. Today we export our products to more than 45 countries across the globe. We can proudly claim that we are an 'Indian Company with Global Standards'.

Our persistent passion for delivering international quality food has brought us even closer to our consumers. Having successfully launched our products in the retail market, we are now expanding our presence on B2C platforms. We aim to democratize our delicacies, which are made from international quality ingredients and suited to the Indian palate.
